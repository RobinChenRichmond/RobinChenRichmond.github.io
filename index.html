Signal Modulation Classification Model

During my first semester at Cornell (2017 fall), Yuena, Hari and me helped solve AMC (Automatic Modulation Classification) problem for MITRE Corporation from Machine Learning approaches.
We worked in Python with Keras library and try solving the problem from two approaches, and finally my new model improved the accuracy by 6% in total and by 12% for normal data.
